Projects

Shades

Made with a team of two other classmates, Shades is a messaging app similar to the Instagram messaging feature. You are able to add friends either with their usernames or with a unique code that can be generated. Messages Can be sent and unsent, as well as images and link previews. Shades was coded with Swift, and used Firebase to Store the data of the encrypted messages and the user information.

MeloLounge

Made with a team of three other classmates, MeloLounge is used to play music and share music. Similar to Spotify, users are able to create and share playlists of songs within the app using chatrooms, save or download songs, upload songs into their accounts, and edit songs metadata. Users can also add and remove friends by username.
